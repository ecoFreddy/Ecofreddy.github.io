Welcome to Fred Fu's page

Fred Fu was born in Santa Cruz, California, and grew up in Guangzhou, China. During his childhood, he lived near the South China National Botanical Garden, a place he visited thousands of times. These early experiences fostered his appreciation for nature and inspired his long-standing interest in ecosystems and the environment.

He received his B.S. in Ecology from Zhejiang University and is currently a research-based master’s student at the University of Florida under the guidance of Dr. Jiangxiao Qiu.

His research interests include agroecosystem and ecosystem modeling, global change ecology, landscape ecology, and artificial intelligence. His current project integrates process-based models, Earth observation data, and machine learning to understand and predict how management practices shape grassland productivity and environmental responses.
